## Why This Matters

Validators can choose which UIDs to evaluate. Making yourself attractive to them is crucial for getting tested and earning rewards.

## What Validators Care About (Priority Order)

### 1. Reliability (40% weight)
- **99%+ Uptime**: Validators skip miners that are frequently offline
- **Consistent Performance**: Always respond, no timeouts
- **Stable Response Times**: Predictable behavior
- **Auto-Recovery**: Restart on crashes automatically

### 2. Response Quality (30% weight)
- **Accurate Classification**: Correctly identify task types
- **High-Quality Actions**: Actionable, specific, complete
- **Fast Responses**: <1 second response time
- **Error Handling**: Graceful failures, no crashes

### 3. Community Engagement (20% weight)
- **Active Participation**: Join Discord/Telegram
- **Professional Communication**: Helpful, not pushy
- **Transparency**: Share metrics and progress
- **Responsiveness**: Answer questions, help others

### 4. Performance Metrics (10% weight)
- **Success Rate**: >95% is good
- **Response Speed**: <1s is excellent
- **Uptime Stats**: 99%+ is ideal
- **Error Rate**: <5% is acceptable
